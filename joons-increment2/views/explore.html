Mission Statement

MonoMuse is dedicated to exploring the relationship between art, technology, and human creativity. The museum invites visitors to experience innovative exhibitions that highlight how digital tools and creative expression can shape the future of culture.

Who We Serve

MonoMuse serves students, artists, families, researchers, and anyone interested in the connection between design, media, and technology.

Founding Year

MonoMuse was founded in 2016.

Milestones

  • 2016 - MonoMuse opens with its first digital storytelling exhibition.
  • 2019 - The museum expands its interactive media lab for visitors.
  • 2023 - MonoMuse gains international recognition for creative technology exhibits.
